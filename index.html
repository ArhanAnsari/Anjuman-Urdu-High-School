Welcome to Anjuman Urdu High School and Jr. College, Shegaon

Our mission is to provide the quality basic education as well as higher education to the students belonging to the minorities as well as other weaker section of the society by creating the scientific and broad human outlook among the students belonging to the poor family to make them self -sufficient and self-reliant and to enable them for serving the nation and global society.

About Our School

The institution is founded by the forefathers of Memon family in 1938 but due to the lack of resources and inadequate atmosphere it has to stop the educational work in 1948.
But in 1958 a great young devoted person Late Alhaj Abdul Sattar Ibrahim seth kacchi from the memon family arise with full courage having the pious spirit of social service to impart the education.
The institution is flourishing and spreading the knowledge and education not only to the people of Shegaon but also to the resource less students coming from the villages adjacent to the town.
The Institution runs the school from Ist standard to 12th standard. The infrastructure of the school is catering the educational needs of the students from the shegaon and vicinity.
The institution has got the energetic and selfless leader like Mohd Rafique seth Memon Sb who has made the radical changes in the organization with his hard work and devotion. The School is turned into the jr college of science in 2000 and it has started the Arts section in 2005. It empowered the people coming from the poor background. It has given the opportunity to those students who have to quit the education due to the poor financial condition.
