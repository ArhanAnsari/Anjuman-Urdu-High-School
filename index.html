Welcome to Anjuman Urdu High School and Jr. College, Shegaon

Our mission is to provide quality education to our students and help them achieve their goals in a nurturing and supportive environment.

About Our School

Anjuman Urdu High School and Jr. College has been a beacon of knowledge and learning in our community for over 50 years. We are dedicated to fostering academic excellence, creativity, and critical thinking in our students.

Our Values

  • Commitment to Academic Excellence
  • Respect for Diversity
  • Integrity and Responsibility
  • Community Engagement
  • Continuous Improvement
